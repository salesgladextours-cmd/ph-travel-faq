Ano ang pinaka-magandang oras para sa island hopping?NEW
▼

Morning departures (7–8 AM) are strongly recommended.

  • Calmer waters and better light for photos in the morning
  • Big Lagoon entry slots fill up fast — early birds get the best experience
  • Afternoon winds can make the boat ride rougher
💡 Advise guests to arrive at the meeting point on time. Late arrivals may miss their boat slot!

13
Pwede bang mag-combine ng dalawang island hopping tours sa isang araw?NEW
▼

Generally, one tour per day is recommended. Each tour takes approximately 5–7 hours including travel time.

  • Back-to-back tours in one day is exhausting and not recommended
  • For guests wanting multiple tours, suggest extending stay to 4D3N or 5D4N
💡 Upsell Opportunity: Suggest a longer stay so guests can experience Tour A + Tour C or Tour B on separate days!
